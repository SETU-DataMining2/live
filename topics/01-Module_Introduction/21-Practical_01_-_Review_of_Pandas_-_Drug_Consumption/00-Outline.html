Review of Pandas - Drug_Consumption

To start this module we have a quiz-based practical to review your pandas skills using a dataset that you may have analysed in your third assignment in Data Mining 1 with Bernard last semester.

Also for the purpose of this practical we will assume that:

  • The only legal drugs are Alcohol, Caffeine, Chocolate, and Nicotine; so all others are classified as illegal.
  • A non-drug user is one who has never used a drug; so has label CL0.
  • A recent user is one who used a drug within the last year; so has label CL3, CL4, CL5, or CL6. Otherwise they have label CL0 (for never used) or CL1 or CL2 for used over a decade ago and for used over a year ago respectively.
